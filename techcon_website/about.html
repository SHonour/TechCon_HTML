Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small regional event aimed at bringing developers and tech enthusiasts together. Over the years, it has grown into an international conference attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. With each edition, TechCon has consistently showcased breakthrough innovations and fostered collaboration within the tech community.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the next generation of technologists through knowledge-sharing, networking, and exposure to cutting-edge technologies. We are committed to diversity, inclusion, and innovation — driving meaningful conversations that shape the future of tech.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Aisha Bello – A renowned AI researcher and ethics advocate. She delivered the 2022 keynote on responsible AI design.

Tom Lim – A cybersecurity expert and author who led the 2021 session on zero-trust architecture and securing distributed systems.
